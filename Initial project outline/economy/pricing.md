## 2. RECURRING COSTS

### Full-Time Salaries (Daily)
*Paid at End of Day. Unpaid = Morale Hit / Leaving.*
- **Peasant/Novice**: 5-10G
- **Seasoned (Lvl 3-5)**: 15-25G
- **Veteran (Lvl 6+)**: 30-50G

### Freelancer Rates
*Paid Upfront + % Cut.*
- **Upfront Fee**: 20G - 80G (Depends on Rank)
- **Reward Cut**: 20% - 50% (Depends on Trait/Greed)
- *Note: Freelancers do NOT charge Daily Salary.*
