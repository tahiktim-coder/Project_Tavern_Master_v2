# WEEK 1-3 PRE-GENERATED CHARACTERS

**Purpose**: Curated adventurer pool for testing and narrative depth  
**Inspiration**: Anime/games (Frieren, JJK, Dark Souls, Berserk, etc.)  
**Total Characters**: 21 (7 per week)  
**Design Goal**: Mix of basic, mysterious, weird, and deeply curious characters

---

## üé≠ DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

**Mix Distribution**:
- **40% Reliable/Basic** - Standard adventurers, good for early quests
- **30% Mysterious** - Hidden depths, secret pasts, unlock special interactions
- **20% Weird** - Quirky, unsettling, or comedic
- **10% Deep/Legendary** - Endgame-caliber, expensive, game-changing

**Progression Curve**:
- Week 1: Mostly F-D rank, 1-2 suspicious characters
- Week 2: D-C rank, introduce first "legendary" character
- Week 3: Mix of all ranks, more mysteries surface
